About

I focus on developer security at Anthropic, working to make AI systems safer and more reliable.

Previously, I led security engineering at Lightspark and managed supply chain security infrastructure at Snap, where my team hardened dependency management, CI/CD pipelines, secrets management, and sandboxing of untrusted code.

Earlier in my career, I was part of the Chrome Security team at Google, focusing on Web Platform Security and usable security.

I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science from UC Berkeley, where I was advised by Dawn Song and researched web application security. Check out WebBlaze for more of our group's work.

Recent Publications

A Week to Remember: The Impact of Browser Warning Storage Policies

Joel Weinberger, Adrienne Felt

SOUPS, 2016

PDF Slides
When someone decides to ignore an HTTPS error warning, how long should the browser remember that decision? We evaluated six storage policies with a large-scale, multi-month field experiment and found that one achieved more of our goals than the rest. Google Chrome 45 adopted our proposal, and it has proved successful since deployed.
