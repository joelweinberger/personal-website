About

I'm a security engineer and researcher currently at Anthropic. Previously, I led security engineering at Lightspark and managed supply chain security at Snap. Before that, I was on the Chrome Security team at Google.

I hold a Ph.D. from UC Berkeley, where I worked with Dawn Song on web application security. My research focused on making the web safer through better security policies and analysis tools. You can find our group's work at WebBlaze.

I earned my B.S. and M.S. from Brown University, and spent time at Sun Microsystems and Microsoft Research along the way.

A Week to Remember: The Impact of Browser Warning Storage Policies

with Adrienne Felt · SOUPS 2016

PDF Slides
How long should a browser remember when someone ignores an HTTPS warning? We ran a large-scale field experiment comparing six storage policies. Chrome 45 adopted our recommendation.

A Systematic Analysis of XSS Sanitization in Web Application Frameworks

with Saxena, Akhawe, Finifter, Song · ESORICS 2011

PDF Slides
We systematically evaluated XSS sanitization in 14 major web frameworks and found significant gaps between what frameworks provide and what applications need.
